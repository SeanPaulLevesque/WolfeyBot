Weight Convention

All actions start at weight = 1.0. Every module multiplies — never adds to — the weight. This keeps vetoes clean, bonuses composable, and the scale intuitive. Only relative ordering between actions matters; there is no normalisation.
> 1.0encouraged
= 1.0neutral / no opinion
< 1.0discouraged
= 0.0vetoed (never pick)

Module Reference

① DAMAGE OUTPUT all moves

DamageOutputModule

Scores every move by the average damage fraction it deals to the best available target. Sets action.target_slot to whichever opponent was most susceptible. Uses the median damage roll (×0.925).

ConditionMultiplier
OHKO (100% avg damage)×3.00
50% avg damage×2.00
25% avg damage×1.50
0% (status / immune)×1.00
Formula: 1 + fraction × 2
② THREAT ELIMINATION KO bonus

ThreatEliminationModule

Stacks a large additional multiplier on top of DamageOutput when a move can guarantee or threaten a knockout. Also overrides target_slot to point at the KO target.

ConditionMultiplier
Guaranteed OHKO (min roll ≥ defender HP)×5.00
OHKO on max roll only×2.50
2HKO (2× avg ≥ HP)×1.50
Combined with DamageOutput: guaranteed OHKO typically reaches
weight ≈ 3.0 × 5.0 = 15–22
③ OPP PROTECT RECENCY attack redirect

OppProtectRecencyModule

Consecutive Protect has a dramatically lower success rate in Gen 9. When target_slot points at an opponent who used a Protect-family move last turn, the attack is unlikely to be wasted — reward it with a small boost.

ConditionMultiplier
Target used Protect/Detect/etc. last turn×1.30
④ PRIORITY / SPEED priority moves only

PrioritySpeedModule

Adjusts the value of priority moves (prio > 0) based on the speed matchup. Priority is most valuable when the opponent may move first; wasted when we already outspeed everyone.

ConditionMultiplier
Any opponent may outspeed us×1.50
We outspeed all opponents×0.80
Fake Out available (first turn in play)×3.00 additional
⑤ PROTECT Protect-family only

ProtectModule

Boosts Protect-family moves when threatened or at low HP. Strongly penalises back-to-back Protect use. Suppresses all bonuses in a 1v1 endgame (last mon alive, one opponent) where Protecting just delays the loss.

ConditionMultiplier
Opponent can OHKO on max roll×2.50
Critical HP (< 5%)×3.00
Low HP (< 25%)×1.50
Used Protect last turn (consecutive)×0.10
1v1 endgame (last mon, 1 opp)no bonus — stays ×1.00
Critical HP waives consecutive penalty, except in 1v1 endgame
⑥ FAKE OUT threat discount

FakeOutModule

When a known Fake Out user (Incineroar, Scream Tail, etc.) is active and has not yet moved this field entry, it will flinch exactly one of our mons. Protect safely dodges it; attacks risk being wasted.

ConditionMultiplier
Protect-family move×3.00
Attack move×0.50
Switch action×1.00 (sidesteps Fake Out)
Calibrated so a confirmed OHKO (≈22) ≈ Protect's combined score (≈11)
⑦ FIELD CONDITION Protect-family only

FieldConditionModule

Boosts Protect on the final active turn of a harmful field effect so the bot stalls rather than attacking into a disadvantageous condition. Strong enough to beat typical damage scores but weaker than a confirmed OHKO.

ConditionMultiplier
Last turn of opponent Tailwind (1 turn left)×3.00
Last turn of active Trick Room (1 turn left)×3.00
Both can stack if both expire simultaneously
⑧ SWITCH switch actions only

SwitchModule

Evaluates bench switches by how well the incoming mon handles the active opponent threat types. Scale is lifted when the current mon is OHKO-threatened, making good escapes competitive with Protect. Vetoes duplicates to prevent both slots switching to the same target.

ConditionMultiplier
OHKO escape → resists threats (≤0.5×)×4.00
OHKO escape → neutral to threats×2.00
Normal → resists threats (≤0.5×)×1.80
Normal → neutral to threats×1.20
Switch target also OHKO-threatenedcap ×0.80
Switch target weak to threats×0.40–0.50
Partner already switching there×0 (veto)
⑨ DOUBLING UP runs last — reads partner decision

DoublingUpModule

Penalises both slots targeting the same opponent (doubling up on a Protect wastes both moves). Reads the partner slot's committed target_slot. Only fires when two or more opponents are active. Spread moves are excluded.

Confirmed-OHKO redirect: when the partner already has a confirmed OHKO on the shared target AND another opponent is alive, attacks are redirected to the surviving opponent rather than near-vetoed — preventing the engine from defaulting to Protect.

ScenarioMultiplier
Doubling up — both factors
(target Protected last turn and other opp not threatening) 		×0.85
Doubling up — one factor
(Protected last turn or other opp not threatening) 		×0.70
Doubling up — neither factor
(Protect risk, other opp is threatening) 		×0.55
Partner near-KO on same target (weight ≥ 10) ×0.65 additional
Partner confirmed OHKO (weight ≥ 15) + alt target exists redirect to alt target, no penalty
Partner confirmed OHKO (weight ≥ 15) + no alt target ×0.05 (near-veto)
